Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 3, 2026

MyFaithWalk ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

MyFaithWalk is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information to provide and improve our services:

  • Data stored on your device: Journal entries, prayer requests, saved verses, streaks, and preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data.
  • Chat messages: When you use the chat feature, your messages are sent to our server to generate responses. We do not store or log these conversations.
  • Subscription data: If you subscribe to premium features, your purchase is processed by Apple (App Store). We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status. We do not collect or store your payment information.
  • Notifications: If you enable notifications, scheduling is handled entirely on your device. We do not use push notification servers or track notification interactions.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not require account creation or login
  • We do not collect your name, email address, or phone number
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not use third-party analytics or tracking tools
  • We do not display advertisements
  • We do not sell, share, or trade any user data

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat: For subscription management. RevenueCat may collect anonymous purchase data. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.
  • OpenAI: Chat responses are generated using OpenAI's API through our server. Messages are processed but not stored. See OpenAI's Privacy Policy.
  • ElevenLabs: Used for voice audio generation. See ElevenLabs' Privacy Policy.

Data Storage and Security

All personal data (journal entries, prayers, favorites, preferences) is stored locally on your device. We do not maintain servers that store your personal content. If you delete the app, all locally stored data is permanently removed.

Children's Privacy

MyFaithWalk does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages, but in-app purchases require parental authorization.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@faithwalkapp.com.
